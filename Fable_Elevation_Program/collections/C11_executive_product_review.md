# COLLECTION C11 — Executive Product Review (acquirer-grade critique)

**Objective:** The critique a venture-backed product team or a strategic acquirer
(Apple / a GameSheet-scale platform) would produce. Ties the whole program together
into a board-ready view.

**Inputs:** every audit produced by S01–S10 and C01–C10; `BUILD_PRIORITY.md`;
`05_WHAT_WINNING_LOOKS_LIKE.md`; the live app at rinkd.app.

**Scope:**
- **Per-screen scorecard roll-up:** consolidate every screen's grade (A+…D) into one
  board view; show the trend as sprints land.
- **The six Winning outcomes:** measure the app against each (register in <3 min,
  goal in <5s, game→stream in <10s, schedule in minutes, portable identity, partners
  see us as complementary). Grade each; name the gap to close it.
- **Investor/acquirer lens:** what would a diligence team flag on design,
  performance, accessibility, and scalability? What's the strongest "this is a
  platform, not a feature" evidence?
- **Risk register:** the top things that would make an operator or investor hesitate,
  ranked, with the sprint that closes each.

**Deliverable:** `audits/C11_executive_review.md` — a concise, honest board memo:
scorecard roll-up, Winning-outcomes grades, diligence-flag list, and risk register.
This is the document you can hand to an investor or a partner exec. **Guardrail:**
brutally honest; no vanity grades.
