# Rinkd Commissioner Guide

**As a commissioner, you run the whole league on Rinkd — from creating it and adding teams, through building the season schedule, all the way to generating the playoff bracket and closing out the season.**

You have full control over everything. Team managers can help you manage their individual teams, but only you can edit the league, manage the schedule, and handle the playoff bracket.

---

## Part 1 — Build your league from scratch

### Step 1: Create the league

1. Sign in to Rinkd at **rinkd.app**.
2. Tap **Leagues** in the bottom navigation.
3. Tap **+ Create** in the top-right corner.
4. Fill in the league details:

   - **League Name** — e.g., "KOHA Winter League 2026"
   - **Division** — e.g., "Oldtimers" or "Adult Rec"
   - **Level** — e.g., "Recreational" or "Competitive"
   - **Location** — the city or rink the league runs out of
   - **Season** — e.g., "Winter 2026" or "Fall 2026"
   - **Logo Initials** — 2–3 letters that appear on the league badge (e.g., "KOHA")
   - **Logo Color** — pick a color for the league badge

5. Tap **Create League**.

Your league is created and you are automatically set as commissioner.

---

### Step 2: Add your teams

1. From the league management screen, tap the **Roster** tab.
2. Tap **Add Team**.
3. Enter a **Team Name** and optionally assign a **Division** if your league has multiple divisions.
4. Tap **Save**.
5. Repeat for each team in the league.

> **Linking to an existing Rinkd team?** If a team already exists on Rinkd (they have their own team page and players), you can link them instead of creating a standalone entry. Tap **Link Existing Team** and search by team name or handle. Linked teams carry their own roster and branding.

> **Not sure of all your teams yet?** Add what you have and come back. You can add, edit, or remove teams at any time before the season starts.

---

### Step 3: Add team managers

Each team can have a manager — someone with elevated permissions on their team page who can update their roster and communicate with their players.

1. From the **Roster** tab, tap the team you want to assign a manager to.
2. Tap **Add Manager**.
3. Type the person's **@handle** or **email address**.
4. Tap **Add**.

They now have manager access for that team. They can manage their roster but cannot edit the league, schedule, or other teams.

> **Note:** You can add managers before or after the season starts. Managers do not need to be added before you go live.

---

### Step 4: Build the schedule

1. From the league management screen, tap the **Schedule** tab.
2. Tap **⚡ Build Schedule** to open the Schedule Builder.
3. In the Schedule Builder, fill in:
   - **Season start date** and **end date**
   - **Game days** — which days of the week games are played (e.g., Tuesdays and Thursdays)
   - **Game times** — the time slots available each game day
   - **Rink / Facility** — where games are played
4. Tap **Generate** and Rinkd builds a round-robin schedule across your teams.

> **Prefer to build it manually?** Tap **Add Game** in the Schedule tab and fill in the details one game at a time — teams, date, time, and rink.

> **Want to edit a game after it's generated?** Tap any game in the Schedule tab to change its time, date, rink, or teams.

---

### Step 5: Go live — make the league public

When you're ready for players and the public to see your league:

1. Tap the **Settings** tab in the league management screen.
2. Find the **Visibility** toggle.
3. Switch it to **Public**.
4. Tap **Save Settings**.

The league is now publicly visible. Players can find it on Rinkd and follow it to get push notifications when games go final.

> **Need to keep it private a bit longer?** Leave it set to Private until you're ready. The league is only visible to you until you flip it.

---

### Step 6: Share the league with your players

1. Open your league page.
2. Copy the URL from your browser — it looks like `rinkd.app/league/[league-id]`.
3. Send that link to your players, captains, and team managers.

Players will need a Rinkd account to follow the league and receive notifications. They can sign up for free at **rinkd.app**.

---

## Part 2 — Managing the season

### Handling join requests

If players or teams request to join your league:

1. Tap the **Requests** tab in the league management screen.
2. You'll see pending requests with the player or team name.
3. Tap **Approve** or **Decline**.

Approved players are added to the league roster. Declined requests are removed from the queue.

---

### Monitoring standings

Standings update automatically the moment a game is finalized. You don't need to do anything.

1. Tap **Leagues** and open your league.
2. Tap the **Standings** tab to see live rankings at any time.

---

### If a scorer made a mistake on a finalized game

Only a commissioner can reopen a finalized game.

1. Tap the **Schedule** tab and find the game.
2. Tap **Open Scorer View**.
3. Tap **🔓 Reopen Game** at the top and confirm.
4. Tell the scorer the game is now open — they can correct the goal log or score.
5. The scorer taps **Finalize** again when corrections are done. Standings update automatically.

---

## Part 3 — Playoffs

### Generate the playoff bracket

Once the regular season is complete and standings are final:

1. From the league management screen, tap the **Schedule** tab.
2. Tap **🏆 Generate Playoffs**.
3. Fill in:
   - **First game date and time**
   - **Game length (minutes)**
   - **Rink** — which sheet playoff games will be on
4. Tap **Generate**.

Rinkd seeds teams based on standings and creates the playoff schedule automatically. As each game finalizes, the bracket advances the correct teams.

> **Shootout or overtime?** If a playoff game ends in a tie, the scorer will see a prompt to enter the overtime or shootout winner before finalizing. The bracket resolves correctly from there.

---

### Close out the season

When the playoff champion is crowned and the season is done:

1. Tap the **Settings** tab in the league management screen.
2. Find the **Status** field.
3. Set it to **Complete**.
4. Tap **Save Settings**.

The league stays publicly visible as a historical record. Standings and results are locked in.

---

## Part 4 — Standalone how-tos

### Add a team after the season has started

1. Tap the **Roster** tab in the league management screen.
2. Tap **Add Team**.
3. Fill in the team name and any division info.
4. Tap **Save**.

You'll need to manually add games for the new team in the Schedule tab to keep things fair.

---

### Remove a team manager

1. Tap the **Roster** tab and open the team.
2. Find the manager under the team's management section.
3. Tap **Remove Manager** next to their name.

They lose manager access immediately. The team's roster and history are unaffected.

---

### Edit league settings mid-season

1. Tap the **Settings** tab in the league management screen.
2. Update the fields you need — name, location, season, points system, etc.
3. Tap **Save Settings**.

Most settings can be changed at any time. Points system changes only affect games going forward — they do not retroactively change standings.

---

## League lifecycle at a glance

| Stage | What you do |
|---|---|
| Setup | Create league, add teams, add managers, build schedule |
| Pre-season | Make league public, share link with players |
| Regular season | Monitor standings, reopen games if needed, handle requests |
| Playoffs | Generate bracket, scorers run playoff games |
| End of season | Set status to Complete |
