# Rinkd Player Guide

**As a player, you can follow tournaments, post chirps to the feed, stay connected with your teammates, and get notified the moment a game goes final.**

---

## 1. Create your account

1. Open your browser and go to **rinkd.app**.
2. Tap **Sign Up**.
3. Type in your email address and choose a password (at least 8 characters).
4. Tap **Create Account**.
5. Check your inbox for a confirmation email from Rinkd. Open it and click the link inside.
6. You're in. Complete the short onboarding — it asks for your name and a handle (your @username on Rinkd).

[screenshot: sign-up screen]

---

## 2. Set up your profile

1. Tap the **Profile** icon at the bottom of the screen (it looks like a person).
2. Tap **Edit Profile**.
3. Add a profile photo by tapping the camera icon on your avatar.
4. Fill in your name, handle, and any details you want other players to see.
5. Tap **Save**.

[screenshot: edit profile screen]

---

## 3. Post a chirp

Chirps are your posts on Rinkd — think of them like a message to the whole hockey community.

1. Tap the **Chirps** icon at the bottom of the screen (it looks like a speech bubble).
2. Tap **Drop a Chirp →** at the top of the feed.
3. Type your chirp in the text box. You have up to 500 characters.
4. To add a photo, tap the camera icon below the text box. Choose a photo from your camera roll.
5. Want to tag your chirp? Tap a tag like **Hot Take** or **Game Recap** to label it.
6. Tap **Post**.

Your chirp appears at the top of the feed right away.

[screenshot: chirp composer with photo option]

---

## 4. Follow another player

1. Tap **Discover** in the bottom navigation (the magnifying glass icon).
2. Tap the **Players** tab.
3. Type a name or @handle into the search bar.
4. Tap the player's name to open their profile.
5. Tap **Follow**.

Their chirps will now show up in your **Following** feed.

[screenshot: player profile with Follow button]

---

## 5. Follow a tournament

Following a tournament is how you get notified when games go final.

1. Tap **Tournaments** in the bottom navigation.
2. Find the tournament you want — active tournaments show a **● Live** badge.
3. Tap the tournament name to open it.
4. Tap the **🔔 Follow** button near the top of the page.
5. Your phone will ask for permission to send you notifications. Tap **Allow**.

From now on, every time a game in that tournament goes final, you'll get a push notification with the final score. Tap the notification to open the game recap.

> **Note:** If you tap **Follow** but deny the notification prompt, Rinkd will still remember that you follow the tournament. You can turn notifications back on later from your Profile settings.

[screenshot: tournament page with Follow button]

## 6. Get push notifications working

### On Android
Push notifications work in any browser on Android. Just make sure you tapped **Allow** when Rinkd asked for permission. If you missed that prompt, go to **Settings → Notifications → Rinkd** on your phone and turn them on.

### On iPhone (iOS)
Apple only sends push notifications when the app is installed on your home screen — opening Rinkd in Safari alone isn't enough.

To install Rinkd to your home screen:

1. Open **rinkd.app** in Safari on your iPhone.
2. Tap the **Share** button at the bottom of the screen (the box with an arrow pointing up).
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Tap **Add** in the top-right corner.

Now open Rinkd from the icon on your home screen, go to a tournament, tap **🔔 Follow**, and allow notifications when prompted.

[screenshot: iOS Add to Home Screen prompt]

## 7. Find a tournament and view live standings

1. Tap **Tournaments** in the bottom navigation.
2. Tournaments marked **● Live** are happening right now.
3. Tap a tournament to open it.
4. The **Standings** tab shows the current rankings for each pool.
5. Tap the **Schedule** tab to see all the games and times.
6. Tap any game to open the full scoresheet.

You do not need to be signed in to view a tournament's public page — but you do need an account to see live standings and scores.

[screenshot: tournament standings tab]

---

## 8. View the tournament feed

1. Open a tournament you follow.
2. Tap the **Feed** tab (it sits between the Bracket tab and the Info tab).
3. Game recaps appear here automatically when a scorer finalizes a game.
4. You can post your own chirps to the tournament feed too — use the text box at the top of the Feed tab.

[screenshot: tournament Feed tab with recap post]

---

## 9. Report a post or block a user

If you see a chirp that shouldn't be there:

1. Tap the **⋯** (three dots) menu in the corner of any post.
2. Tap **Report** to flag it. Choose a reason from the list and add any details.
3. Tap **Send**. The post disappears from your view and goes to Rinkd's moderation queue.

If you want to stop seeing posts from a specific person entirely:

1. Tap **⋯** on any of their posts.
2. Tap **Block @handle**.
3. Confirm the block. You will no longer see their posts, and they will no longer see yours.

To manage your blocked users, go to **Profile → Settings → Blocked Users**.

[screenshot: post action menu showing Report and Block options]

---

## 10. Sign out

1. Tap **Profile** in the bottom navigation.
2. Scroll down and tap **More**.
3. Tap **Sign Out**.

---

## Quick reference

| What you want to do | Where to find it |
|---|---|
| Post a chirp | Chirps tab → Drop a Chirp |
| Find players to follow | Discover → Players tab |
| Follow a tournament | Tournaments tab → open tournament → 🔔 Follow |
| View live standings | Tournaments tab → open tournament → Standings tab |
| Report a post | ⋯ on any post → Report |
| Block a user | ⋯ on any post → Block @handle |
| Enable notifications (iPhone) | Install Rinkd to home screen first |
