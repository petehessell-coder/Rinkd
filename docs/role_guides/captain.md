# Rinkd Captain Guide

**As a captain, you can build and manage your team on Rinkd — add players to the roster, post to the team feed, RSVP on the team's behalf, and run everything from your phone instead of a group chat.**

Everything in the Player Guide applies to you too. This guide picks up from there and covers the captain-specific tools.

---

## 1. Create a team

1. Tap **Teams** in the bottom navigation (the jersey icon).
2. Tap **+ Create Team**.
3. Enter your team name.
4. Tap **Create**.

You are now the team manager. Your name is the contact on the team page.

[screenshot: Create Team screen]

---

## 2. Set your team logo and colors

1. Open your team page by tapping **Teams** and then your team name.
2. Tap **Manage Team** (the gear or settings button near the top).
3. Tap **Edit Team**.
4. To add a logo, tap **Upload Logo** and choose a photo from your camera roll. Keep it under 5 MB.
5. To pick a team color, tap the color swatch and choose from the palette.
6. Tap **Save**.

[screenshot: Edit Team screen with logo upload and color picker]

---

## 3. Invite players to your team

You can add players by their Rinkd handle or by email.

### By @handle
1. On your team page, tap **Manage Team**.
2. Tap **Roster**.
3. Tap **Add Player**.
4. Type the player's @handle into the search box.
5. Tap their name when it appears.
6. Tap **Send Invite**.

They'll get a notification and can accept or decline.

### By email
1. Follow steps 1–3 above.
2. Type an email address instead of a handle.
3. Tap **Send Invite**.

The player will receive an email with a link to join your team on Rinkd.

[screenshot: Add Player screen with handle/email input]

---

## 4. Approve or deny a join request

Players can also request to join your team on their own.

1. Tap **Teams** and open your team page.
2. Tap **Manage Team**.
3. Tap **Roster**.
4. Under **Pending Requests**, you'll see anyone who has asked to join.
5. Tap **Approve** to add them to the roster.
6. Tap **Deny** to decline the request.

[screenshot: Pending Requests list with Approve/Deny buttons]

---

## 5. Remove a player from the roster

1. Open your team page and tap **Manage Team → Roster**.
2. Find the player you want to remove.
3. Tap the **⋯** next to their name.
4. Tap **Remove from Team**.
5. Confirm.

[screenshot: roster list with remove option]

---

## 6. Post to the team feed

The team feed is a private chirp board — only your roster can see it.

1. Open your team page.
2. Tap the **Feed** tab.
3. Tap the text box at the top that says **What's the chirp?**
4. Type your message. You can add a photo by tapping the camera icon.
5. Tap **Post**.

Your teammates will see it on the team feed.

[screenshot: team feed with composer at top]

---

## 7. RSVP for a game

When your team has a game on the schedule, you can confirm attendance for yourself and track who's coming.

1. Open your team page and tap the **Schedule** tab.
2. Find the upcoming game.
3. Tap the game to open it.
4. Tap **RSVP** and choose **In** or **Out**.

Players on the roster can RSVP themselves. You can see the full RSVP list from the game page.

[screenshot: game detail with RSVP buttons]

## Quick reference

| What you want to do | Where to find it |
|---|---|
| Create a team | Teams tab → + Create Team |
| Upload a team logo | Teams → Manage Team → Edit Team |
| Invite a player | Teams → Manage Team → Roster → Add Player |
| Approve a join request | Teams → Manage Team → Roster → Pending Requests |
| Post to team feed | Teams → your team → Feed tab |
| RSVP for a game | Teams → your team → Schedule tab → tap game |
